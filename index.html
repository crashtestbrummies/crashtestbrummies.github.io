Roller Derby Fresh Meat - Brummies Taster Event

Do you love to skate, always wanted to skate...not skated for years? Do you love full contact sports? Do you live in or close to Birmingham? The Crash Test Brummies are holding an open introduction event on Sunday 2nd September for anyone who is interested in learning about the wonderful sport of Roller Derby. We are looking at taking on our next batch of 'Newbies' after a very successful intake in 2016 and 2017, our coaches are keen for some 'Fresh Meat'. So if you fancy a new challenge we would love to hear from you. We currently have players in their 20's, 30's, 40's and yes a few in their 50's! (You must be a least 18 years of age) The correct protection equipment will be provided if you have skates fantastic, if not let us know your size in advance and we should hopefully be able to provide...What have you got to loose? Be there or be really boring! The Villain ;)

Home Coming Scrim - Return to Brum

To celebrate the Crash Test Brummies return to the Second City and back to our old training venue Leasowes, we are holding an 'Open Scrim' on Sunday 10th June from 4pm - 7pm. Doors will open at 4pm and on track at 4:30pm...First Whistle at 5pm. We would love all our old friends who've skated here before to come along, and extend an invite any new friends too. Just £5 on the door, please bring Black/White Scrim Tops and prepare for a 'Smashing Time'. Open to all 'Mins Passed' skaters. If any lovely Official and NSO's are available please let us know. Look forward to seeing you on Track! By Order of the Peaky Brummies

FAQs

When/Where do you train?
Sunday evenings 6:30-9:30pm at Leasowes High School, Kent Rd, Halesowen, West Midlands, B62 8PJ
How much does it cost?
Your first session is free. Following that, each session is £5.
I can't skate or haven't skated in years, can I play?
Fear not, we will teach you how to skate and put you through our ‘fresh meat’ programme to get you up to speed.
What equipment do I need?
We can provide you with the gear to get you started. It is not a good idea to splash out on your own gear before deciding roller derby is for you. Please let us know your size requirements in advance.
